MARKETING

Job Roles

Business Development Executive(BDE)

Responsibilities:

  • Find And Seize New Business Chances In The Website Design And Digital Marketing Industries.
  • Establish And Preserve A Solid Rapport With Clients By Getting To Know Their Needs And Offering Specialized Solutions.
  • Create And Implement Strategic Plans To Meet Revenue Growth Objectives And Sales Targets.
  • Work Together With The Marketing Team To Develop Campaigns And Collateral That Are Successful.
  • Keep Abreast Of Market Developments And Rivalry To Spot Openings And Prospective Growth Opportunities.
  • To Find And Evaluate Potential Customers, Conduct Market Research.
  • Make Sure Your Presentations Are Polished And Delivered To Potential Customers.

Qualifications

  • Demonstrated Expertise In Business Development In The Fields Of Website Design And Digital Marketing.
  • Strong Grasp Of Website Building, Digital Marketing Tactics, And Related Technology.
  • Outstanding Interpersonal And Communication Abilities, With The Capacity To Establish And Preserve Client Relationships.
  • Demonstrated Ability To Reach And Surpass Sales Goals.
  • Understanding Of Consumer Behavior In The Digital Sphere, Competitor Analysis, And Market Trends.
  • A Goal-Oriented Mentality That Emphasizes Achieving Objectives And Fostering Corporate Expansion.
  • A Bachelor’s Degree In Marketing, Business, Or A Similar Discipline.
  • Proficiency With Other Pertinent Business Development Tools And CRM Applications.
  • Utilizing Creative Thinking And Problem-Solving Abilities, Novel Solutions Can Be Developed For Clients.
  • Capacity To Operate Both Individually And Cooperatively In A Team.

Business Development Manager(BDM)

Responsibilities:

  • Create And Execute Well-Thought-Out Business Development Strategies To Propel The Expansion Of Digital Marketing And Website Design Services.
  • To Meet Revenue Goals, Find And Seize New Business Possibilities, Build And Nurture Customer Relationships, And Negotiate Contracts.
  • Work Together With Cross-Functional Teams To Guarantee Client Satisfaction And Project Delivery That Runs Smoothly.
  • To Take Advantage Of New Opportunities, Keep Up With Market Circumstances, Competition Activity, And Industry Trends.
  • Oversee The Development Of Persuasive Presentations And Proposals To Highlight The Company’s Strengths And Attract New Clients.

Qualifications

  • Demonstrated Expertise In Business Development In The Fields Of Website Design And Digital Marketing.
  • Strong Familiarity With The Newest Trends, Tools, And Best Practices In Digital Marketing.
  • Outstanding Interpersonal And Communication Abilities To Interact And Establish Rapport With Clients.
  • Proven Capacity To Inspire And Guide Groups Of People To Meet Goals For Both Sales And Project Quality.
  • A Strategic Mentality Combined With Analytical And Problem-Solving Skills.
  • A Bachelor’s Degree In Marketing, Business, Or A Similar Discipline; A Graduate Degree Is Preferred.
  • Proficiency With Project Management Tools And CRM Applications.

On-Site Marketing

Responsibilities:

  • Create And Implement On-Site Marketing Plans To Improve Our Digital Marketing And Website Design Services’ Internet Visibility.
  • Make Sure Your Website Is Search Engine Optimized For Optimal Visibility And Interaction.
  • Work Together With Cross-Functional Teams To Develop Unified And Effective On-Site Marketing Initiatives.
  • Track And Evaluate Performance Indicators For Websites, Offering Analysis And Suggestions For Ongoing Development.
  • Use User Experience Optimization And A/B Testing To Increase Lead Generation And Conversion.

Qualifications

  • A Bachelor’s Degree In Business, Marketing, Or A Similar Discipline.
  • A Track Record Of Success In On-Site Marketing For Digital Marketing And Website Design.
  • Strong Familiarity With Website Analytics Tools And SEO Best Practices.
  • Proficiency With Basic HTML/CSS And Content Management Systems, Such As WordPress.
  • Possessing An Inventive Thinking And The Capacity To Produce Creative Concepts For On-Site Campaigns.
  • Outstanding Interpersonal And Teamwork Abilities, With The Capacity To Function Well In A Group Setting.
  • A Results-Oriented Strategy Emphasizing Data-Driven Decision-Making.
  • Knowledge Of The Newest Developments In Digital Marketing Trends And Technologies.
