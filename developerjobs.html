DEVELOPER

Job Roles

Front-End Developer

Responsibilities:

  • Work Together With The Design Team To Develop User-Friendly And Aesthetically Pleasing Website Interfaces.
  • Write HTML, CSS, And JavaScript Code That Is Clear, Effective, And Manageable.
  • Make Sure That Third-Party Apps And Back-End Services Integrate Seamlessly.
  • Websites Should Be Optimized For Optimal Responsiveness And Speed.
  • Investigate And Fix Problems With Cross-Browser Compatibility.
  • To Improve The User Experience, Keep Up With Developing Technology And Market Trends.

Qualifications

  • A Track Record Of Success In Front-End Development Or A Related Field.
  • Competence With JavaScript, HTML, CSS, And Related Frameworks (Such As React, Angular, Or Vue.Js).
  • Solid Knowledge Of The Fundamentals Of Responsive Design.
  • Knowledge Of Version Control Systems, Such As Git.
  • Strong Understanding Of SEO Best Practices And Principles.
  • Good Communication And Problem-Solving Abilities.
  • Capacity To Collaborate In A Deadline-Driven, High-Speed Work Environment.
  • Portfolio Exhibiting Prior Projects And Demonstrable Impact On User Experience.
  • A Bachelor’s Degree In Web Development, Computer Science, Or A Similar Discipline Is Advantageous.

Full-Stack Developer

Responsibilities:

  • Create And Manage User-Friendly, Responsive Websites And Web Apps.
  • Work Together With Cross-Functional Teams To Comprehend Project Specifications And Produce Excellent Results.
  • To Improve Website Performance, Implement And Optimize Both Front-End And Back-End Capabilities.
  • Utilize Analytics And Digital Marketing Tools In Tandem To Monitor And Enhance Website Performance.
  • Keep Abreast With Market Developments And New Technological Advancements In Order To Provide Creative Fixes.

Qualifications

  • Adept In Front-End Technologies, Including JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, And Well-Known Frameworks Like Angular Or React.
  • Strong Proficiency In Server-Side Programming Languages Such As Python, Ruby, Or Node.Js.
  • Familiarity With Database Management Systems Like PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Or MySQL.
  • Familiarity With Agile Development Techniques And Version Control Technologies, Such As Git.
  • The Ability To Apply SEO Best Practices In Site Construction And Knowledge Of Them.
  • Outstanding Ability To Solve Problems And Pay Attention To Details.
  • Proficiency In Communication To Effectively Work With Teams In Design, Marketing, And Development

Web Designer

Responsibilities:

  • Design And Develop User-Friendly, Aesthetically Pleasing Websites That Support Companies’ Brands And Marketing Goals.
  • Work Together With Internal Teams And Clients To Comprehend Project Requirements And Convert Them Into Visually Appealing Website Designs.
  • To Guarantee The Delivery Of Cutting-Edge And Inventive Online Solutions, Stay Current On Design Trends, Tools, And Technology.
  • Improve A Website’s Responsiveness, Performance, And Cross-Browser Compatibility.
  • To Integrate Design Components And Guarantee Seamless Integration, Collaborate Closely With Developers.

Qualifications

  • Demonstrated Expertise As A Web Designer And A Robust Portfolio Featuring A Variety Of Imaginative And Creative Online Projects.
  • Competent With Programs Like Sketch, Adobe Creative Suite, And Other Graphic Design Software.
  • Solid Knowledge Of JavaScript, HTML, And CSS To Interact With Developers In An Efficient Manner.
  • Understanding SEO Best Practices And Principles In Order To Support Website Optimization Initiatives.
  • Possessing Outstanding Communication Abilities And The Capacity To Work With Cross-Functional Teams And Clients.
  • Meticulous And With A Strong Sense Of Aesthetics And User Experience.
  • Capacity To Oversee Several Projects And Fulfill Deadlines In A Hectic Setting.
  • Preferably, You Have A Bachelor’s Degree In Graphic Design, Web Design, Or A Similar Profession.

UI/UX Designer

Responsibilities:

  • Work Together With Cross-Functional Teams To Comprehend User Requirements And Corporate Objectives For Projects Including Digital Marketing And Website Design.
  • Utilize The Newest Design Tools, User Experience Approaches, And Industry-Leading Design Concepts To Create Aesthetically Pleasing And Intuitive User Interfaces.
  • To Effectively Convey Design Concepts And Interactions, Create High-Fidelity Designs, Prototypes, And Wireframes.
  • Perform Usability Testing And User Research To Obtain Insightful Input For Ongoing Digital Asset Improvement.
  • Assure Design Uniformity Across A Range Of Platforms And Gadgets To Preserve Corporate Identification And Raise Customer Happiness.
  • To Add Creative And Avant-Garde Solutions To Projects, Stay Current On New Design Trends, Techniques, And Technology.

Qualifications

  • A Bachelor’s Degree In Graphic Design, Human-Computer Interaction, Or A Similar Discipline, Along With A Solid Portfolio Of Completed UI/UX Design Work.
  • Competence With Industry-Standard Design Programs Like Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch, Figma, Or Other Programs Of A Similar Nature.
  • Strong Knowledge Of Information Architecture, Interface Design, And User-Centered Design Principles.
  • Proficiency In Developing Responsive Designs And Enhancing User Interfaces For Various Screen Sizes And Devices.
  • Excellent Communication Abilities To Work With Cross-Functional Teams And Effectively Communicate Design Concepts.
  • Ability To Operate Effectively In A Deadline-Driven, Fast-Paced Atmosphere And Swiftly Iterate In Response To Feedback.
  • It’s Advantageous To Have Knowledge About Digital Marketing Theories And Tactics.
  • A Dedication To Providing Outstanding User Experiences And A Desire For Remaining Current With Design Trends.

Mobile App Developer(Android)

Responsibilities:

  • Create And Implement Top-Notch Android Mobile Applications In Accordance With Project Specifications And Business Guidelines.
  • Work Together With Cross-Functional Teams To Comprehend User Requirements, Project Objectives, And The Integration Of Digital Marketing Techniques Into The Creation Of Mobile Apps.
  • Assure Application Responsiveness, Quality, And Performance While Fixing Errors And Putting Effective Fixes In Place.
  • Keep Abreast Of New Developments In Mobile App Development Tools, Trends, And Technologies, And Incorporate Them Into The Company’s Initiatives.
  • For The Best User Experience, Collaborate Closely With UX/UI Designers To Develop User Interfaces That Are Visually Appealing And Intuitive.

Qualifications

  • A Bachelor’s Degree In Software Engineering, Computer Science, Or A Similar Discipline.
  • Demonstrated Expertise Developing Android Apps, With A Solid Portfolio Of Apps That Have Been Released With Success.
  • Competent In The Programming Languages Java And Kotlin, And Well-Versed In The Android SDK And Development Tools.
  • Knowledge Of Digital Marketing Theories And Tactics And The Capacity To Apply Them To The Creation Of Apps That Will Increase User Interaction.
  • Strong Problem-Solving Abilities, Meticulousness, And A Proactive Approach To Mobile Application Optimization And Troubleshooting.
  • Understanding Of Best Practices For Mobile Apps And Responsive Design Ideas.
  • Strong Teamwork And Communication Abilities To Function Well In A Group Setting.
  • The Capacity To Change With Changing Industry Trends And Technology.
  • It Is Advantageous To Have Knowledge Of Version Control Systems Like Git.

Mobile App Developer(IOS)

Responsibilities:

  • For Our Clients, We Create And Manage Superior IOS Applications That Meet Their Demands For Digital Marketing And Website Design.
  • Work With Cross-Functional Teams To Convert Design Concepts Into Aesthetically Pleasing And Intuitive IOS Applications.
  • Keep Up Of The Most Recent Advancements In IOS Development Trends And Technology To Guarantee The Incorporation Of Cutting-Edge Functionality.
  • Improve The Functionality, Responsiveness, And User Experience Of Mobile Apps.
  • Carry Out Comprehensive Testing And Debugging To Find And Fix Problems Quickly.
  • To Smoothly Include Mobile Solutions Into Overall Strategy, Collaborate Closely With The Teams In Charge Of Website Design And Digital Marketing.

Qualifications

  • A Solid Portfolio Of Apps That Have Been Released With Success And Demonstrated Expertise In Developing IOS Applications.
  • Proficiency With The Programming Languages Swift And Objective-C As Well As A Thorough Knowledge Of IOS Frameworks.
  • Knowledge Of Third-Party Libraries, Online Service Integration, And RESTful APIs.
  • Strong Problem-Solving Abilities And Meticulousness In Application Development And Coding.
  • Capacity To Communicate Technical Ideas To Stakeholders Who Are Not Technical And Work Well In Cross-Functional Teams.
  • Current Understanding Of Best Practices, New Trends, And IOS Design Requirements.
  • A Bachelor’s Degree In Software Engineering, Computer Science, Or A Similar Discipline.
