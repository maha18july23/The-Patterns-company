What is Google-Ads ?

Google Ads is an online advertising platform developed by Google, designed to help businesses promote their products and services through targeted advertisements. It operates on a pay-per-click (PPC) model, where advertisers bid on keywords relevant to their target audience. These ads can appear on Google's search engine results pages (SERPs), websites within the Google Display Network, YouTube videos, and other partner sites. Google Ads offers a variety of ad formats, including text ads, display ads, video ads, and app promotion ads, allowing advertisers to reach potential customers across different channels and devices. Through precise targeting options and robust analytics, Google Ads enables businesses to effectively reach their desired audience, drive website traffic, and achieve their marketing objectives.
